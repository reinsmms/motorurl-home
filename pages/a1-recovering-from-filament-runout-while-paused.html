Recovering from Filament Runout When the A1 Is Already Paused

Applies to Bambu Lab A1 printers without AMS.

Symptoms

  • Printer shows Paused
  • Filament menu only shows Edit
  • Load / Unload options are missing

Why this happens

The A1 only enables Load and Unload during idle states or when a filament change is explicitly expected.

Recommended recovery

  1. Tap Resume
  2. Allow extrusion attempt
  3. Runout is detected and printer re-pauses
  4. Load new filament and resume

If runout is not detected

  1. Resume the print
  2. Gently pull remaining filament past the sensor
  3. Printer enters filament-change mode

What not to do

  • Do not cancel the print
  • Do not power-cycle
  • Do not force filament while paused
